=====<Movelist>=====

-- Button Assign
x - Light Punch
y - Hard Punch
z - none
a - Light Kick
b - Hard Kick
c - none
start - Taunt


-- Moves
Front Step - F, F
Back Step - B, B
GC Attack - y+b while guarding
Safe Fall Recovery - x+a when nearing ground while falling


-- Throw
Seoi Nage - F, y

Tomoe Nage - F, b


-- Command Moves
Seichu Nidan Tsuki - F, x

Zyoudan Sokutou Geri - F, a


-- Super Moves
Hadou Ken - D, DF, F, x or y

Shouryu Ken - F, D, DF, x or y

Tatsumaki Senpu Kyaku - D, DB, B, a or b(can use in air)

Shakunetsu Hadou Ken - B, DB, D, DF, F, x or y

Ashura Senku - F, DF, D, x+y or a+b(Forward) / B, DB, D, x+y or a+b(Back)


-- DM
Shinku Hadou Ken - D, DF, F, D, DF, F, x or y

Shinku Tatsumaki Senpu Kyaku - D, DB, B, D, DB, B, a or b

Messatsu Gou Shouryu - D, DF, F, D, DF, F,  a or b


-- SDM
Shinku Hadou Ken - D, DF, F, D, DF, F, x or y

Shinku Tatsumaki Senpu Kyaku - D, DB, B, D, DB, B, a or b

Messatsu Gou Shouryu - D, DF, F, D, DF, F,  a or b

Shungoku Satsu - (charge)B, F, B, F, x or y

Ryukoku Hadou Ken - D, DF, F, D, DF, F, a or b
