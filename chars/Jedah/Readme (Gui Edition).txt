 The bloody boss from Vampire Savior.

=====<Features>=====

 - All the essential stuff
 - Mordenized POTS Style gameplay
 - Details and moves taken from his Vampire Savior and Capcom Fighting Jam appearances
 - Gameplay mixed from several games, including CvS2, SFZ3, Darksltakers and SF3
 - Good amount of intros and win poses
 - Special victory pose against several Fatal Fury chars
 - Midnight Bliss and other special animations
 - New palettes
 - Quotes
 - No A.I., but he's compatible with Patches that people around done before



=====<Movelist>=====

 U - up          x - weak punch         a - weak kick
 D - down        y - medium punch       b - medium kick
 F - forward     z - strong punch       c - strong kick
 B - back        p - any punch          k - any kick
 s - start       2p- two punches        2k- two kicks

 (Air)      - Move must be performed ONLY in the air.
 (Air Also) - Move must be performed in the ground and in the air.
 (EX)       - Move has an EX version, performed by pressing two punch/kick buttons.
 (MAX)      - Use two punch/kick buttons when performing a Super move to power it up.

<SYSTEM>

.Forward Fly:                     F, F (hold)
.Backward Fly:                    B, B (hold)
.Low Jump:                        tap U
.High Jump:                       tap D, U
.Long Low Jump:                   tap D, tap U            
.Sidestep:                        a + x
   .Sidestep Attack:              p / k
.Forward Roll:                    F + a + x
.Backward Roll:                   B + a + x
.Parry				  tap F
.Parry High:                      tap D
.Air Parry:                       tap F           (Air)
.Power Charge:                    hold b+y
.Zero Counter:                    F, D, DF, p     (during standing or crouching guard)
.Custom Combo:                    c + z           (Air also)
.Fast Recovery:                   tap any button
